Simon Game!

Game Instructions

1. Check the "ON/OFF" box.

2. Increase game difficulty by checking the "Strict" box to choose Strict Mode.

3. Click on "Start"

4. Once "Start" has been activated the game will begin.

5. Your challenge is to match the blinking colors in the right order.

6. To win the game you have to go through all 20 levels.
